Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a demo take?

Our demos typically last 30 minutes and can be customized to focus on your specific research needs and use cases.

Can I bring my own data?

Yes! We encourage you to bring your own data for a more personalized demonstration. We'll analyze it in real-time during the demo.

Is BiomixX suitable for my research area?

BiomixX is designed for researchers across all life sciences disciplines. We currently support proteomics and are expanding to other omics technologies.

How quickly can I get started?

After your demo, you can typically get started within 24-48 hours. We provide onboarding support to ensure you get the most out of the platform.
